Enable your microphone; wait for your popcorn to come to a rolling pop (multiple pops per second); quite all non-popping noise; get this device close to your popcorn (within "earshot"); hit the "Start" button below. Once it hears 2 examples of the time between pops exceeding 1.5 seconds, it will let you know by displaying a message and changing the background color of this screen.

Fun Fact: Most microwave's use a timer or mosture detector when you hit "popcorn." Only a minority use auditory systems like this.
